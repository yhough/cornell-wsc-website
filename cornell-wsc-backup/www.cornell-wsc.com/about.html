About WSC

What is WSC?

  • Accelerating Careers in Finance — WSC empowers members to enter competitive financial sectors through structured preparation and strategic resources.

  • Expansive Professional Network — Members gain access to a global community of industry leaders, alumni, and top-tier financial institutions.

  • Targeted Mentorship & Career Development — Personalized mentorship and skill-building initiatives equip members with the expertise to excel in recruiting and beyond.

  • Exclusive Industry Access — Through workshops, speaker panels, and firm partnerships, members engage directly with top finance professionals.

What do we offer?

WSC offers two signature mentorship programs designed to prepare members for success in finance. The Wall Street Mentorship builds technical and analytical skills, while the Interview Preparation Mentorship focuses on communication, strategy, and confidence in recruiting.

Mentorship programs

  • WSC Wall Street Mentorship

    Our Wall Street Mentorship Program is structured to provide members with a rigorous, end-to-end foundation in finance fundamentals, valuation, and core deal processes, with an emphasis on translating theory into practical, interview-ready skill sets. The program begins with an intensive three-week introductory finance series, followed by four weeks of focused investment banking training covering valuation, transaction dynamics, and case-based technical preparation. Members then advance into a four-week Sales & Trading track, where they develop market fluency through weekly workshops, market and macro frameworks, trade idea formation, and practical simulations that mirror the pace and decision-making of real trading environments. Delivered through weekly workshops, case studies, and technical training led by experienced mentors and alumni, the program is designed to build both competence and confidence for recruiting, internships, and long-term success across investment banking and markets roles.

  • WSC Interview Preperation

    The Interview Preparation Mentorship helps members develop the communication, problem-solving, and analytical skills essential for finance recruiting. Participants receive one-on-one feedback, mock interview sessions, and exposure to real interview formats used by leading firms. By combining technical mastery with personalized guidance, the program ensures every member is ready to perform at their best in both behavioral and technical interviews.
