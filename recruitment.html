Process

What to Expect

1

Application

Submit a short written application covering your background, interests in finance, and why you want to join WSC.

2

Coffee Chats

Selected applicants are invited to informal coffee chats with current members to learn more about the club and share their goals.

3

First Round Interview

Selected candidates complete a first round interview covering finance fundamentals, motivation, and initial fit with the WSC community.

4

Final Round Interview

Top candidates from the first round are invited back for a final interview, going deeper into technical knowledge and cultural fit.

5

Offers

Successful candidates receive an offer and are welcomed into the club, with immediate access to mentorship and programming.
