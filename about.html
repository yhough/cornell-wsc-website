About WSC

Accelerating careers in finance through structured preparation, mentorship, and strategic resources.

Who we are

Cornell Wall Street Club

WSC is Cornell's premier finance club, dedicated to accelerating members' careers through structured education, mentorship, and direct access to the financial industry. We bring together ambitious students and connect them with the people, knowledge, and opportunities needed to break into Wall Street.

Accelerating Careers

Structured preparation and strategic resources to help members land roles in competitive financial sectors.

Expansive Network

Access to a global community of industry leaders, alumni, and top-tier financial institutions.

Targeted Mentorship

Personalized mentorship and skill-building to excel in recruiting and long-term career development.

Industry Access

Workshops, speaker panels, and firm partnerships that put members in direct contact with finance professionals.

Mentorship Programs

Two signature programs designed to take members from fundamentals to offer.

Wall Street Mentorship

A rigorous, end-to-end foundation in finance fundamentals, valuation, and core deal processes — translating theory into practical, interview-ready skill sets through weekly workshops, case studies, and training led by experienced mentors and alumni.

  • Core Financial & Valuation Concepts
  • Financial Modeling Essentials
  • Industry Applications
  • Accounting & Financial Statement Analysis
  • Leveraged Buyouts (LBOs)
  • Advanced Deal Analysis

Interview Preparation Mentorship

Develops the communication, problem-solving, and analytical skills essential for finance recruiting. Members receive one-on-one feedback, mock interview sessions, and exposure to real formats used by leading firms.

  • Professional Development & Recruiting Strategy
  • Behavioral & Fit Interview Training
  • Projects, Practice & Peer Development
