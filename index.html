What We Do

Mentorship Education

Each new member completes our Wall Street and Interview Preparation mentorships to develop technical expertise and career readiness.

Networking & Opportunities

WSC empowers members to build lasting professional relationships and pursue high-impact career opportunities across the finance industry.

Speaker Series

Our Speaker Series features accomplished leaders from top firms who share real-world insights on finance, careers, and markets.

NYC Trips

We host exclusive visits to New York's leading financial institutions, providing firsthand insight into Wall Street's culture while fostering connections with alumni and peer chapters across universities.
