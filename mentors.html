Lexi Friesel is a junior from Atherton, California, majoring in Economics with a minor in Business at Cornell University. On campus, she is a member of the NCAA Women's Polo Team, Senior Advisor for the Society for Women in Business, a Big Red Ambassador for Admissions, a contributor to the Big Red Sports Network, and a photographer for the Cornell Football Team.

Lexi previously interned at Oak Hill Advisors in Private Credit and at Searchlight Capital Partners in Private Equity. This summer, she will be an Investment Banking Summer Analyst at Evercore.

In her free time, Lexi enjoys lifting weights, pilates, skiing, watching sports, listening to music, traveling the world, and exploring new restaurants. She can be reached at ahf58@cornell.edu
